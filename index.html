Quality Signals 0/8 ›

Observe before you ask. Pick up a sample and run through this list silently.

Q1 Stitch density 8–10 SPI?Count on a seam. Below 6 = walk away. Critical
Q2 Bartack at every stress point?Strap root, D-ring base, handle attachment. Dense rectangular stitch block.
Q3 Interior seams bound or taped?Open the bag. Raw fraying edges = cheap. Bound seams = quality.
Q4 Hardware feels solid and heavy?Pick up the zip pull. Tap it. Metal rings. Plastic thuds. Light + hollow = cheap alloy.
Q5 Bag stands upright empty?Put it on the floor. Quality structure + metal feet = stands. Collapses = no internal integrity.
Q6 Front panel stays flat under gentle tension?Pull lightly. Bubbles or wrinkles = lining too thin. Flat = quality construction.
Q7 Care label printed cleanly?Crisp, precise text = quality control. Blurry or off-register = production sloppiness.
Q8 Complex interior sample available to inspect?Ask: "Show me the most complex interior you've produced." Their answer = truth.
